================================================================================
HOTKEYS
================================================================================
Alt+Shift     - Toggle language (auto-detected)
Ctrl+`        - Undo last fix OR Force-fix unfixed word
Ctrl+Alt+E    - Force English mode
Ctrl+Alt+H    - Force Hebrew mode
Ctrl+Alt+A    - About dialog
Ctrl+Alt+Q    - Quit


================================================================================
FILES
================================================================================
config.ini         - Settings (block delay)
ignore_words.txt   - Words to never auto-correct
learned_words.txt  - Words you taught via Ctrl+` force-fix
hebrew_words.txt   - Hebrew dictionary (470,000 words)
align_left.png     - Outlook align button image
align_right.png    - Outlook align button image
switcher_log.txt   - Debug log (only when using run_debug.bat)
